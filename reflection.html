Group Reflection

How we thought we worked together as a group.

Group Reflection

The major factors that worked well among our group in order to carry out our assigned tasks successfully and efficiently were delegation of work, time management, and cooperation. Each meeting would have an agenda where we would look through the assignment specification and understand what work needed to be accomplished using Microsoft Teams. Our team split our work into subtopics, with certain individuals focusing on specialized duties, such as Ideal Jobs, Industry Data, IT Work, IT Technologies, and so on. In terms of time management, our team worked consistently from start to end, with no instances of last-minute rushed work. Finally, another major strength of our team was cooperation. There were no disagreements or conflicts over the duty that had been given to each member; we were all satisfied with the task that had been assigned to us. In terms of coding, GitHub was really beneficial since two of our members set up the GitHub repository so that others can quickly update their work using GitHub Desktop, team members only have to commit the work, and everyone can view it.

The main aspect needing improvement was remembering the meeting times as once our group member forgets about the meeting time but as we messaged him, he was online within 2-3 minutes. Also, as some of our group members are working it takes time for them to respond. This can be improved by having everyone check their notifications and messages throughout the day. We also need to work on our communication skills to let everyone know about the problem they are facing such as clashing with the assignment. However, as a team, we are certain that we can overcome this issue in future projects and work toward achieving our common objective of producing high-quality work.

The surprising part for our group was the level of devotion shown by each and every member of our group in order to achieve our deadline and develop realistic and doable concepts for our project. The fact that we satisfied the rubric's standards by reading it thoroughly and making certain that all elements of the assignment were well-completed was another important factor.

The most important thing we learnt as a group was that working in a team - based environment helped us empower creativity, establish mutual trust, and learn methods to tackle challenges by coming up with a solution as a group.

Reflection on GitHub

Using GitHub in a group was surprisingly easy and stress-free. At first it did seem a bit daunting having multiple people uploading at once, but working on individual sections definitely made it a lot easier to work with. Everyone played their part on GitHub and assisted with the finalisation of the website which was great to see.

There didn’t really seem to be too many issues with how the website was working and whatnot and GitHub’s pushing, pushing functions through GitHub Desktop made it extremely easy and straightforward.

Everyone’s contributions towards the repository also made it extremely easy. The use of comments and having titles that reflected the section that they were working on was extremely helpful, alongside with comments as everyone was pushing to the repo, so we were continually updated with what was being completed and why it was being pushed to the origin.
